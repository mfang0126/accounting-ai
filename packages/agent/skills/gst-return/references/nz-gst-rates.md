# NZ GST Rate Reference

## Current Rates (2026)

| Rate | Applies to |
|------|-----------|
| 15% | Standard rate — most goods and services |
| 0% | Zero-rated — exports, sale of going concern, certain financial services |
| Exempt | Not a taxable supply — residential rent, financial services (interest), donated goods |

## GST Calculation Methods

**Tax-exclusive (adding GST):**
GST amount = net price × 0.15
Total (GST-inclusive) = net price × 1.15

**Tax-inclusive (extracting GST):**
GST amount = total price × 3/23
Net price = total price × 20/23

## Filing Periods

| Type | Periods | Who |
|------|---------|-----|
| Monthly | Jan, Feb, Mar... | Turnover > $24M or by choice |
| 2-monthly | Jan-Feb, Mar-Apr, May-Jun... | Most common, default |
| 6-monthly | Apr-Sep, Oct-Mar | Turnover < $500K, by choice |

## Payment Due Dates

- Standard: 28th of the month following period end
- March period: extended to 7 May (alignment with income tax)
- If due date falls on weekend/holiday: next business day
